OUR STORY

MISSION

My Healthy Shop was created with the purpose of making it easier to lead a healthy life. Our products are 100% organic, not processed and without additives, preservatives or added sugars. We like to offer food that is produced responsibly, which means that we support farmers, local producers and artisans who use sustainable methods. We believe in offering everyone the ability to eat well, feel good and live a better life.

VALUES

Eat good At My Healthy store, we believe that today, more than ever, eating well is the best medicine to have a healthy life, for this it is essential to have local producers. Feel good Nourish your body and your mind. Live more and live better. Celebrate good things with friends and family. Do good Create a local community, support farmers who use sustainable methods to make this project last for many years. Because the most important thing is to feel good
